update()

The first four parts of the following update() method check whether the ball has reached the edge of the canvas. If it has, we reverse the polarity of the relevant velocity to make the ball travel in the opposite direction. So for example, if the ball was traveling upwards (positive velY), then the vertical velocity is changed so that it starts to travel downwards instead (negative velY).
